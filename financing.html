Choose Your Financing Path

Cash Purchase

Pay upfront and own your system immediately

Pros:

  • No interest payments
  • Maximum savings
  • Full ownership

Considerations:

  • • High upfront cost
  • • Large initial investment

Solar Loan

Finance your system with competitive rates

Pros:

  • Low monthly payments
  • Own your system
  • Tax benefits

Considerations:

  • • Interest payments
  • • Credit requirements

Solar Lease

Start with low payments and eventually own

Pros:

  • Low initial cost
  • Flexible terms
  • Maintenance included

Considerations:

  • • Higher total cost
  • • Longer payback period

Power Purchase Agreement

Pay only for the power you use

Pros:

  • No upfront cost
  • Immediate savings
  • Maintenance included

Considerations:

  • • Don't own system
  • • Long-term contract
